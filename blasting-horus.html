The Company

A technology company focused on stone scanning, whether marble, granite, ceramics, onyx, sandstone, limestone or slate. recognized the need to unify all its products into a cohesive ecosystem, with the aim of improving operational efficiency, simplifying processes and offering a unified user experience. The goal is to create a tenant that centralizes and integrates all of the company's products in an intuitive, user-friendly platform.

Analysis

Analysis of Existing Products: The company's 8 existing products were analyzed, identifying gaps, redundancies and integration opportunities.

User Journey Mapping: I mapped out user journeys to identify touchpoints and interactions.

Prototypes: I created prototypes to visualize the tenant's information architecture and navigation flow.

Visual Design and Brand Identity: All of the company's visual identity was maintained in the creation of the ecosystem, so that everything is very cohesive, ensuring consistency and brand recognition across all products.

Mobile x Desktop

This product has both mobile and desktop versions, adaptable to the needs of different contexts of use and designed to offer a user experience regardless of the platform used.

Phase 3: Develop

Quick Prototyping: We used rapid prototyping techniques to turn ideas into tangible prototypes that could be easily implemented by the development team.

Development of High-Fidelity Prototypes: Based on feedback, we developed high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and tested the feasibility of the designs with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap5, to try to optimize the development team's time. This process reflected the final design of the CRM system.

Phase 4: Delivery

Feedback Integration: We incorporated user feedback into the final prototypes, ensuring that the product met the users' needs and expectations.
